Poison Dart Frogs

"Poison dart frogs are the world’s most brightly colored frogs, and some are the most poisonous animals on earth. Different species are red, orange, yellow, green, blue, black, or some combination. These bright colors and elaborate designs are considered warning or aposematic coloration. This signals to potential predators that these animals are dangerous and won’t be a good meal. Other frogs mimic the poison dart frog’s coloration to try to confuse predators and avoid being eaten themselves."
